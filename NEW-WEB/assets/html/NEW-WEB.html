What is space?

The word "cosmos" is translated from Greek as "order", "system", "orderliness". In ancient times, this is what the world structure was called - that is, it was about Order as opposed to Chaos. Now space is everything that exists outside the Earth's atmosphere. Such a concept of outer space assumes its infinity.
